About

Martin Campbell is an avid photography hobbyist with a lifelong passion for astronomy and photography. Combining his two great loves, Martin captures the breathtaking beauty of the night sky from various locations around the world. His work, celebrated in exhibitions globally, reflects his dedication to revealing the celestial wonders that inspire awe and curiosity. Whether photographing the Milky Way over remote deserts or capturing star trails in serene countrysides, Martin's images invite viewers to explore the universe through his lens.

Astro-photographer from Dungannon & avid Guinness enjoyer.

Martin Campbell holds a Master’s degree in Geography, a testament to his deep understanding of the Earth's landscapes and natural phenomena. This academic background has enriched his perspective as an astro-photographer, allowing him to capture the intricate relationship between the Earth's surface and the night sky. His geographical expertise lends a unique depth to his photographic compositions, merging the beauty of our planet with the mysteries of the cosmos.

Martin's lifelong interest in astronomy has been the driving force behind his photographic journey. From a young age, he was fascinated by the stars, planets, and the vastness of the universe. This passion led him to pursue astro-photography, where he could combine his love for astronomy with his skill in capturing stunning visuals. His work has taken him to some of the best stargazing locations worldwide, including the clear skies of France, the arid landscapes of Chile, the remote outback of Australia, and the serene countryside of Donegal, Ireland.

Before dedicating himself full-time to photography, Martin was a beloved teacher at St. Patrick's Academy in Dungannon. His time at the academy allowed him to inspire countless students with his enthusiasm for geography and astronomy. His travels for astro-photography have not only enriched his own experience but have also provided invaluable educational content, which he often shares through lectures and exhibitions. Martin's journey is a blend of education, exploration, and a relentless pursuit of capturing the universe's beauty.
